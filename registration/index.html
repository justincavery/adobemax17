Education, government, and non-profit

Full conference pass pricing for education, government and the non-profit community is $995, regardless of purchase date. A promotion code is required for the discount. When registering, use Education code: 17MED or Government code: 17MGV or Non-profit code: 17MNP. An education, government, or non-profit email address is required. Note: only one discount code can be applied and promotion code discounts apply to the full conference pass price of $1,595.

Students

Full conference pass pricing for full-time students, 18 years or older, who are enrolled at a higher education institution is $299, regardless of purchase date. Students must bring a copy of their class schedule showing at least 12 credits or units and a valid student ID as proof of your student enrollment. When registering, use Student code: 17STU.

Group passes

Group pricing is available to companies when they purchase 3 or more full conference passes in one transaction. This does not apply to preconference bundles.

To receive this rate, the purchaser needs to create an account in the MAX registration system and on step 3 of registration, select Group Registration Package and select your number of passes from the drop down menu. Once purchased, your confirmation email will have a group registration code that your team members may use to complete their registration.
